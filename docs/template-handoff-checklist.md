# Work Handoff Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist ensures smooth transitions when work is handed off between team members, roles, or project phases. It promotes knowledge transfer, reduces rework, and maintains project continuity.

---

## Pre-Handoff Preparation

- [ ] Schedule handoff meeting with sufficient time for questions
- [ ] Prepare documentation and artifacts for transfer
- [ ] Identify critical information and context that must be transferred
- [ ] Review current status and outstanding items
- [ ] Notify relevant stakeholders of upcoming handoff

---

## Documentation Transfer

- [ ] Share all relevant documentation (specs, designs, plans, etc.)
- [ ] Provide access to necessary repositories and files
- [ ] Share location of templates and resources used
- [ ] Document any undocumented decisions or context
- [ ] Share meeting notes and communication history
- [ ] Provide links to related issues, PRs, or tickets

---

## Context and Background

- [ ] Explain project/work item objectives and success criteria
- [ ] Share historical context and key decisions made
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders and their interests
- [ ] Clarify scope and boundaries
- [ ] Review acceptance criteria and definition of done
- [ ] Explain any constraints or dependencies

---

## Current Status

- [ ] Review completed work and achievements
- [ ] Identify work in progress and current state
- [ ] Highlight upcoming tasks and priorities
- [ ] Discuss known blockers or risks
- [ ] Share current timeline and milestones
- [ ] Review any pending decisions or approvals

---

## Technical/Functional Details

- [ ] Explain technical architecture or design approach (if applicable)
- [ ] Share code locations, branch names, or deployment details
- [ ] Demonstrate key features or functionality
- [ ] Review test coverage and quality standards
- [ ] Explain any technical debt or known issues
- [ ] Share configuration, environment, or setup details

---

## Stakeholder and Communication

- [ ] Introduce to key stakeholders and their roles
- [ ] Share communication channels and cadence
- [ ] Explain escalation paths and decision-making process
- [ ] Review stakeholder expectations and concerns
- [ ] Transfer ongoing communication threads or updates
- [ ] Update stakeholders about handoff and new point of contact

---

## Tools and Access

- [ ] Verify access to necessary tools and systems
- [ ] Share credentials or access procedures (securely)
- [ ] Demonstrate tool usage and workflows
- [ ] Provide links to dashboards or monitoring tools
- [ ] Confirm permissions are adequate for role

---

## Knowledge Transfer Session

- [ ] Conduct live walkthrough of current work
- [ ] Answer questions and clarify uncertainties
- [ ] Demonstrate processes and workflows
- [ ] Share tips, tricks, and lessons learned
- [ ] Discuss potential challenges and how to address them
- [ ] Provide contact information for follow-up questions

---

## Follow-Up and Support

- [ ] Define follow-up support period and availability
- [ ] Schedule check-in meeting after initial transition
- [ ] Establish communication channel for questions
- [ ] Offer to review early work or decisions
- [ ] Agree on when full ownership transfers

---

## Role-Specific Handoff Items

### Developer to Developer
- [ ] Review code architecture and design patterns
- [ ] Share local setup and development workflow
- [ ] Explain testing strategy and CI/CD pipeline
- [ ] Highlight any technical debt or refactoring needs
- [ ] Review coding standards and conventions used

### Product Owner Transition
- [ ] Transfer product backlog with context for priorities
- [ ] Share stakeholder relationships and communication history
- [ ] Review product vision and roadmap
- [ ] Explain metrics and success criteria
- [ ] Introduce to key customers or user representatives

### QA Lead Handoff
- [ ] Share test plans, test cases, and coverage reports
- [ ] Explain testing environments and data setup
- [ ] Review open defects and their priorities
- [ ] Share quality metrics and trends
- [ ] Explain automation framework and tools

### Business Analyst Transition
- [ ] Transfer requirements documentation and traceability
- [ ] Share business process models and workflows
- [ ] Review stakeholder analysis and needs
- [ ] Explain business context and domain knowledge
- [ ] Share analysis tools and data sources

### Project Manager Handoff
- [ ] Transfer project plan, schedule, and milestones
- [ ] Review risk register and mitigation plans
- [ ] Share stakeholder communication plan and history
- [ ] Explain resource allocation and constraints
- [ ] Review budget and financial tracking (if applicable)

### UX/UI Designer Handoff
- [ ] Share design files, prototypes, and assets
- [ ] Explain design decisions and user research findings
- [ ] Review design system and component library
- [ ] Share user feedback and usability test results
- [ ] Explain accessibility requirements and standards

### Change Manager Handoff
- [ ] Transfer change log and pending change requests
- [ ] Share impact assessments and approval history
- [ ] Review change management process and policies
- [ ] Explain stakeholder communication approach
- [ ] Share lessons learned from previous changes

---

## Handoff Completion

- [ ] Recipient confirms understanding and readiness
- [ ] All documentation transferred and accessible
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of transition
- [ ] Follow-up support plan established
- [ ] Handoff meeting notes documented
- [ ] Recipient has assumed full ownership
